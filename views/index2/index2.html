EXPERTS IN FILM: THE AMATEUR INTEREST HOUR

I love movies. I read up on them, study them, and watch a lot of them. I love to talk about movies. I invite experts to come talk about film.


Why are they experts? Because I say so. Our guests are amateurs, sometimes they are experts (but definitely not in film), and occasionally they are legitimate film experts.


Listen in to learn about film from people who seem like they know what they are talking about. People tend to know more about film than they think they know.

JANUARY 15, 2018

The Big Sick with Tanner Talley

Celebrating the start of the Sundance Film Festival, me and my wife, Tanner Talley, review a Sundance hit from last year, The Big Sick! We talk about what makes this movie great, the genre of the Romantic Comedy, and give Ray Romano high praise.

JANUARY 9, 2018

GOVERNOR HERBERT!

Governor Herbert invites the podcast into his office! We talk to him about current Utah issues such as Bears Ears National Monument, the Budget, and the upcoming Legislature! We hear his recommendations for movies and some of his favorites.

JANUARY 4, 2018

Leaving Las Vegas with Marc Nicholas

Fun Fact #1: Nicolas Cage once won an oscar. Fun Fact #2: That movie was called Leaving Las Vegas. Fun Fact #3: Marc Nicholas, my brother, and I watched that movie and HATED it. Come listen and find out why and listen to our discussion on why an oscar nominated film could be terrible and what makes a good movie!

JANUARY 4, 2018

The Wicker Man with David Hart

For the final episode of the Cage before Christmas, we cover the 2006 horror film, The Wicker Man. David Hart stops in and we try to talk about one of the most confusing movies we have seen in a while. Can Nicolas Cage save this trainwreck, or is he the conductor?
