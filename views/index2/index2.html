EXPERTS IN FILM: THE AMATEUR INTEREST HOUR

I love movies. I read up on them, study them, and watch a lot of them. I love to talk about movies. I invite experts to come talk about film.


Why are they experts? Because I say so. Our guests are amateurs, sometimes they are experts (but definitely not in film), and occasionally they are legitimate film experts.


Listen in to learn about film from people who seem like they know what they are talking about. People tend to know more about film than they think they know.
